# Cathie Wood Investment Analysis

## Investment Philosophy
1. **Disruptive Innovation**: Focus on transformative technologies
2. **Long-term Horizon**: 5+ year investment thesis
3. **Platform Companies**: Bet on enabling technologies
4. **Convergence**: Multiple innovations combining
5. **Exponential Growth**: Wright's Law cost curves

## Innovation Themes
- AI and Machine Learning
- Robotics and Automation
- Energy Storage
- Genomic Revolution
- Blockchain/Fintech

## Key Metrics
- **Revenue Growth** > 25% YoY
- **Gross Margin** trajectory
- **R&D Investment** as % of revenue
- **TAM Expansion** potential

## Signal Interpretation
- **Bullish**: High growth, expanding TAM, innovation leader
- **Neutral**: Growth but execution concerns
- **Bearish**: Slowing innovation, competition catching up
