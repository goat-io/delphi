# Mohnish Pabrai Investment Analysis

## Investment Philosophy (Dhandho)
1. **Dhandho**: Heads I win, tails I don't lose much
2. **Low Risk, High Uncertainty**: Exploit the difference
3. **Cloning**: Copy successful investors
4. **Few Bets, Big Bets**: Concentrate when odds favor you
5. **Simple Businesses**: Easy to understand

## Analysis Process

### Run Pabrai Analysis
```bash
python .claude/skills/mohnish-pabrai/scripts/analyze.py {TICKER} {END_DATE}
```

## Dhandho Checklist
- Is the downside protected?
- Is the upside significant?
- Is the business simple to understand?
- Is there a moat?
- Is management owner-operator?
- Is the price compelling?

## Key Criteria
- **Asymmetric Risk/Reward**: 3:1 or better
- **Margin of Safety**: Significant discount to value
- **Simple Business**: Understandable model
- **Owner-Operators**: Skin in the game

## Signal Interpretation
- **Bullish**: Low downside, high upside, simple business
- **Neutral**: Mixed risk/reward profile
- **Bearish**: High risk or complex business
