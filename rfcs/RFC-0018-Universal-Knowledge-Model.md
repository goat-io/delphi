# RFC-0018 — Universal Knowledge Model
## A Unified Representation of Reality, Knowledge, Understanding, Evaluation and Action

Status: Draft (Rewritten after RFC-0019)

Depends On:
- RFC-0000 through RFC-0017
- RFC-0019

---

# Purpose

This RFC answers the most important question in Delphi:

How can every domain use the same protocol?

Examples:

- Physics
- Biology
- Law
- Engineering
- Medicine
- Design
- Business
- Personal Knowledge

Why can all of them coexist inside the same system?

This RFC formalizes the universal model.

---

# Core Principle

Delphi does not model domains.

Delphi models how intelligence constructs, compresses, evaluates, and evolves understanding.

Domains are merely specializations.

---

# The Missing Layer

Traditional knowledge systems model:

Reality
→ Evidence
→ Beliefs

Delphi models:

Reality
→ Evidence
→ Beliefs
→ Understanding

Understanding is represented through Knowledge Indexes.

---

# The Universal Pattern

Every domain eventually contains:

Reality
→ Evidence
→ Beliefs
→ Knowledge Indexes
→ Evaluations
→ Decisions
→ Tasks

This pattern appears everywhere.

---

# Why Knowledge Indexes Matter

Knowledge can become arbitrarily large.

Understanding cannot.

Agents and humans operate through compressed representations.

Therefore:

Knowledge
≠
Understanding

Delphi models both.
